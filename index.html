About me

Gaming in my spare time is something I’ve been doing for the majority of my life, it gives me an opportunity to learn from popular and successful Game Companies by taking inspiration from them, and then trying to implement features from their games into my projects. This way, I can express my own creativity by experimenting with the features, tweaking different mechanics, adding my own twist and making them my own - therefore expanding my library of ideas and adding tricks up my sleeve, as well as learn and develop my programming skills at the same time.

Programming games and solving various computational problems is something I've been passionate about ever since I started playing video games...

Throughout my time in education I have acquired various skills like confidence, leadership, teamwork and communication skills which help boost my ability to work in a team. As well as this, my hard work is something which allows me to excel in my skills, and improve in areas which I want to get better at. Developing games is one of those things; it's something which I can’t get bored of, and look forward to doing in my spare time - not only is it something I enjoy doing, but it's also a career path I would love to take.
